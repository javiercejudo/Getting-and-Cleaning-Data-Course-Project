## Main variables

These are the variables by which all others are averaged. There are 30 subjects and
6 activities, hence 180 rows in the `tidy.txt` dataset.

 - Subject: the ID of the subject
 - Activity: the name of the activity

  - laying
  - sitting
  - standing
  - walking
  - walking downstairs
  - walking upstairs
